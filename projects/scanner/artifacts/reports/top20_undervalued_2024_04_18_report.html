Methodology

1. The source file is the exported S&P 100 undervaluation screen with analysis date 2024-04-18.

3. Portfolio construction is equal-weight, 20 positions, no rebalance, buy-and-hold from the next completed trading session after the screen date.

5. Benchmarks are QQQ and SPY, where SPY is used as the investable proxy for the S&P 500.
